# StatTerrain v0.3.8.2 — National Demographic Benchmarks Handoff

COMPLETE — NATIONAL DEMOGRAPHIC BENCHMARKS ACTIVATED

This patch adds a canonical United States 2024 ACS 5-year benchmark artifact for the seven supported county demographic percentage metrics. Runtime comparison logic preserves county values and emits descriptive percentage-point differences only when release, estimate period, universe, and method are compatible.

United States comparisons are descriptive percentage-point differences and do not establish statistical significance or causation.
